The little secret behind the cookies:

Although our homemade cookie recipe is a secret, here you can find some recommendations if you want to make these delicious cookies at your home:

Use high-quality butter,cheap butter can contain up to 19 percent water, which not only makes it harder for the eggs and butter to emulsify. European butter tends to have lower water content and a higher fat content (which is what you want), as does butter from some small domestic dairies.

Let Your Butter Sit at Room Temperature for 15 Minutes. Fifteen minutes! Not more and not less. If the butter is too cold, it won't cream properly and the resulting cookies will be too dense.
